- Use rubocop-rspec to lint spec tests ([@abrom][])
- Normalisation of PDF conversion options (so they match the expected format/case of Puppeteer) ([@abrom][])

### Fixed
- Lint issues raised by rubocop-rpsec ([@abrom][])

### Changed
